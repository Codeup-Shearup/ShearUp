ShearUp

Why ShearUp?

Because supporting local barbers is important; now, more than ever before. ShearUp was dreamed up at the beginning of the pandemic of 2020. When stay-at-home orders were put into place, it made it near impossible for barbers and clients to unite.

ShearUp makes it easy for barbers and clients to reconnect, or to connect for the first time!

How do I ShearUp?

Barbers

Your clients are your business. Create your own custom profile page where you can share a bit about yourself with clients, such as when and where you work, show off your portfolio of services and styles, and keep track of your clients, both current and new.

Clients

Is that hair getting a little long? Is that beard getting a bit scruffy? Would you REALLY trust someone at home to take those clippers to your head? With ShearUp you can find local barbers, browse their services and styles, and request an appointment right from their profile.

Community

Whether you're a barber or a client, ShearUp aims to build a community of people and revitalize an industry deeply impacted by the pandemic of 2020.
